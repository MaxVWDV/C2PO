About Us

Welcome to the Caius College Polar Observatory (C-2PO). We are dedicated to monitoring and studying the conditions of glaciers and landscapes in the Arctic and Antarctic regions. Our mission is to provide valuable data and insights on climate change and its impacts on these critical environments.

Our team of researchers and students collect and analyze data from satellite imagery and live meteorological conditions. Through our observations, we aim to contribute to the understanding and preservation of polar regions.

Lead by Dr. Max Van Wyk de Vries, Fellow in Geography at Gonville and Caius college. https://www.cai.cam.ac.uk/people/dr-maximillian-van-wyk-de-vries
